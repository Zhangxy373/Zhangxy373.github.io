Projects & Column

I am eager to engage in the development of the open-source community. Although my development skills are still evolving, I have already taken part in several open-source projects. I will continue to actively share my personal projects, hoping to provide some wheels to those in need. Here are some of the projects I've been involved in, along with their descriptions:

  • MUC-CityU_Report_Latex_Template (Github) (Overleaf) : This template is specifically designed for students of the Hainan International College, a joint institution of Minzu University of China and City University of Macau. Given that our two distinguished schools do not offer an official template nor have there been any precedents, I have created this simple template by integrating templates from other schools with my own layout experience.
  • yolo_label_tool (Github) : Here are several custom programs I developed for handling label data in various formats. The goal is to transform these labels into formats acceptable by the YOLO model or to visually preview the labels on images. I found that existing tools do not adequately address certain issues, especially when dealing with segmented images that feature annular labels. To tackle these problems, I created these programs. One technique I use for labels with closed loops involves creating a gap at the thinnest part of the loop, turning it into a polygon. However, this approach may not be suitable for all tasks, so please use it judiciously based on your specific needs.
